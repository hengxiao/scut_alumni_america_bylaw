# Topic: Standing and Special Committees

## Definition

This topic covers the framework for creating and operating committees within the organization. It answers:

- What is the difference between a standing committee and a special (ad hoc) committee?
- Who has the authority to create, modify, or dissolve a committee?
- How are committee members appointed and what are their terms?
- What authority does a committee have, and what decisions remain with the Board?
- How does a committee report its work to the Board and to the membership?
- What records must a committee keep?
- What happens to a special committee once its task is complete?
