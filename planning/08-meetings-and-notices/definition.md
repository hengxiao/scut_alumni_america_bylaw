# Topic: Meetings and Notices

## Definition

This topic covers the rules for convening and conducting meetings at all levels, and for delivering required notices. It answers:

- How often must regular Board meetings and membership meetings be held?
- Who has authority to call a special meeting?
- How far in advance must notice be given, and through what channels?
- What must a notice include (purpose, date, time, location)?
- What constitutes a valid meeting location (physical, virtual, hybrid)?
- How is attendance defined for virtual participants (quorum purposes)?
- What is the agenda process?
- How are meetings adjourned and reconvened?
- What records must be kept of each meeting, and who is responsible?
