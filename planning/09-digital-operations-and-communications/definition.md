# Topic: Digital Operations and Communications

## Definition

This topic covers how the organization manages its online presence, official communications, and digital assets. It answers:

- What are the organization's official communication channels?
- Who controls the official website, email domain, and social media accounts?
- How are access credentials managed and handed off when officers change?
- What constitutes an official organizational communication vs. a personal statement?
- How is member data collected, stored, and protected?
- What digital tools may be used for voting, meetings, and record-keeping?
- Who is responsible for maintaining and archiving digital records?
