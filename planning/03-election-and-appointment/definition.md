# Topic: Leadership / Executive Election and Appointment Process

## Definition

This topic covers how leaders are chosen, replaced, and removed. It answers:

- Who is eligible to run for or be appointed to each role?
- How are candidates nominated?
- Who conducts elections and by what method (in-person, electronic, etc.)?
- What voting thresholds are required to win?
- What is the term of each role, and when do terms begin and end?
- Under what conditions and by what process can an officer be removed or recalled?
- How are vacancies filled mid-term?
- How is a tie resolved?
