# Topic: Dissolution

## Definition

This topic covers the process for winding down the organization and distributing its assets. It answers:

- Under what circumstances may the organization be dissolved?
- Who has the authority to initiate dissolution?
- What vote is required to approve dissolution?
- How are outstanding liabilities settled before asset distribution?
- To whom may remaining assets be distributed, and under what constraints?
- What are the legal requirements for dissolution of a California Non-Profit Mutual Benefit Corporation?
- What notifications are required (members, California Secretary of State, IRS)?
