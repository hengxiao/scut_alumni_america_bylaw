# Topic: Decision Process

## Definition

This topic covers how the organization formally makes decisions at every level. It answers:

- What constitutes a quorum for the Board and for membership meetings?
- What voting thresholds apply to ordinary vs. significant decisions?
- Which decisions require a simple majority, a supermajority, or unanimous consent?
- How are votes cast and recorded (show of hands, secret ballot, electronic)?
- Can members or directors vote by proxy?
- How are tied votes resolved?
- How are decisions documented and communicated?
- What decisions are reserved exclusively for the membership vs. the Board?
