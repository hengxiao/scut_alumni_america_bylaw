# Topic: Leadership / Executive Structure

## Definition

This topic covers the composition of the organization's governing bodies and the relationships between them. It answers:

- What governing bodies exist (e.g., Board of Directors, executive officers)?
- What are the defined roles and titles within each body?
- What is the chain of authority and reporting structure?
- What are the minimum and maximum sizes of each body?
- How do the governing bodies relate to the general membership?
- What are the specific duties and responsibilities of each role?
