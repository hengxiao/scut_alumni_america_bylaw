# Topic: Financial Management

## Definition

This topic covers how the organization's money is collected, held, spent, reported, and reviewed. It answers:

- Where are organizational funds held, and under what name?
- Who is authorized to access and disburse funds?
- What approval is required for expenditures, and at what thresholds?
- What payment methods are authorized (checks, online platforms, etc.)?
- What financial records must be kept, and for how long?
- What financial reporting is owed to the membership and how often?
- Is an independent financial review or audit required?
- How are membership dues and event fees collected and recorded?
- Who oversees the financial process if the Treasurer is unavailable?
