# Topic: Membership and Eligibility

## Definition

This topic covers who belongs to the organization, how membership is established and maintained, and what membership means. It answers:

- Who is eligible to become a member?
- How does one apply for or register as a member?
- Who maintains the official membership roll?
- What are the rights of members (voting, holding office, attending events)?
- What are the obligations of members (fees, conduct, adherence to By-Laws)?
- What are the membership fee structure and payment process?
- Under what conditions does membership lapse or terminate?
- Is there a distinction between active and inactive members?
