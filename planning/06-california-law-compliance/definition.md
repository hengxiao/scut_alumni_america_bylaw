# Topic: California Civil Law Compliance

## Definition

This topic identifies the requirements of California law that apply to the organization as a Non-Profit Mutual Benefit Corporation and ensures the By-Laws reflect them. It answers:

- What does the California Corporations Code (Title 3, Part 3) require for mutual benefit corporations?
- Which statutory provisions must appear in the By-Laws explicitly?
- Which statutory provisions apply automatically and need not be restated?
- Are there filing, registration, or reporting obligations to the California Attorney General or Secretary of State?
- What are the legal requirements for director liability, indemnification, and insurance?
- Are there restrictions on political activity and tax-exempt status under state and federal law?
