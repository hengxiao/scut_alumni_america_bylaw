# Topic: Conflict of Interest

## Definition

This topic covers how the organization identifies and manages situations where a director, officer, or member has a personal interest that may conflict with the interests of the organization. It answers:

- What constitutes a conflict of interest?
- Who is subject to the conflict of interest policy (directors, officers, committee members)?
- How must a conflict be disclosed, and to whom?
- What is the process for deciding whether a conflict is material?
- May an interested party participate in discussion or vote on the matter?
- How is the process and outcome documented?
- What are the consequences of failing to disclose a conflict?
