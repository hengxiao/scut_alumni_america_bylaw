# Amendment Process

The procedure below is defined by **Article VIII** of the current By-Laws and must be followed for any amendment to be valid.

---

## Formal Steps (Article VIII)

### Step 1 — Proposal in Writing (§2)

The proposed amendment must be submitted in writing and signed by either:
- At least **10% of the members**, or
- At least **6 members of the Board of Directors**

### Step 2 — Announcement (§3)

The proposal must be announced to all members **at least 30 days** before the annual membership meeting.

### Step 3 — Discussion at Membership Meeting (§3)

The proposal must be **presented and discussed** at a membership meeting.

### Step 4 — Adoption Vote (§4)

Adoption requires a **two-thirds (2/3) majority vote** of the members present at the membership meeting.

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility in This Process |
|---|---|
| **Drafting committee** | Maintain the rationale and draft documents |
| **Secretary** | Issue formal notices; collect signatures; certify adoption |
| **Chairman** | Convene board review; preside at the membership meeting |
| **Board of Directors** | Review draft; provide signatures; vote |
| **Members** | Participate in discussion; vote at the membership meeting |
